Super Andes BankInBest ATPos SJ Accesorios y Repuestos
Super Andes was a proyect developed for the subject Sistemas Transaccionales, offered at the University of the Andes, based on all the fundamentals of databases, including indexes, logs, isolation levels and more. the project consists on making a marketplace platform that could handle complex queries on an Oracle relational database. BankInBest was the project I realized as Early Bachelor Project. It consisted on a virtual adviser that analyzed all investment projects or stocks available in real time. The project had a complete backend, frontend and a web scrapping / logic independently deployed components, and the "unique" part of this idea was to automatize the process of finantial investment advice and to help people find the best option of investment, with taxes and interests being refreshed every day.

SJ Accesorios y Repuestos its a motorbike replacements marketplace located in Bucaramanga - Santander. this project was developed for an industrial engineering student at the University of Santo Tomas, also located in Bucaramanga.
